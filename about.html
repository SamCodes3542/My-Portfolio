Our Mission

Bridging the employment gap for teens with disabilities by equipping them with the skills necessary to be successful in the workforce

The Butterfly Foundation was created to be a liaison for high school students who have been diagnosed with an array of disabilities such as physical, intellectual, and cognitive disabilities for the workforce by providing training, mentorship, guidance and career services. We do this through interview preparation, on the job training and preparation for things our kids will be experiencing while on the job. We believe that our teens have a great chance at being successful with the right services and job opportunities while they are in high school. The Butterfly Foundation collaborates with businesses to implement on-the-job work training and mentorship programs for individuals with disabilities that may lead to possible employment opportunities in the future. We provide services such as assistance with interview questions, mock interviews, clothing assistance, transportation assistance, job skills, training assistance, and mentorship and volunteer programs for on-the-job experience.
